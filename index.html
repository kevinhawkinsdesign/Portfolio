About

"The best design leaders don't just direct — they make."

I'm Kevin — a gay, Liberian-American design leader based in Barcelona. I've spent 18 years building products and the teams behind them, working across Europe, the US, and Asia. Currently VP of Product Experience at Monta, where I was their first-ever UX hire.

I operate at every altitude: shaping strategy in the boardroom, running workshops with engineers, shipping UI, and building the research practices that make it all smarter. AI-native isn't a buzzword for me — I build with it daily and design products powered by it.

Outside of work I'm the founder of The Common Thread Club (a foodie community of 1,000+ in Barcelona), UXDX Barcelona Ambassador, and a board member at We Create Space. I've visited 52 countries and counting.
