Background

The intersection of science and craft

Kevin Hawkins is a VP of Product Experience based in Barcelona — a city he chose after living and working across Washington D.C., San Francisco, Amsterdam, Sydney, and London. His career sits at the intersection of behavioral psychology, product design, and technical engineering.

He holds a background in Web Design & Interactive Media and studied behavioral psychology — which informs everything from how he runs research sessions to how he designs nudge-based onboarding flows. He spent three years as a front-end software engineer, meaning when he talks to engineering teams, he speaks their language.

As a gay, Liberian-American, he is passionate about building diverse and inclusive teams. He serves on the board of We Create Space, an LGBTQ+ leadership organization in Barcelona, and has spoken on inclusion, design leadership, and behavioral design on stages across Europe and North America.

Over the past five years he has led design organizations of up to 105 people. Before that: product designer, UX researcher, and consultant — building things from zero for startups, federal institutions, and Fortune 500s alike.
