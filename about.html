About

Product UX leader. Team builder.

I've spent 20+ years at the intersection of behavioral science, product design, and business strategy — working with teams that care deeply about getting it right, and building the organizational infrastructure that makes great design repeatable.

My work has always been about more than interfaces. The real design challenge is usually organizational — getting the right people in the room, creating shared language between design, product, and engineering, and building the systems that let teams move fast without losing quality.

I started my career as a product designer and UX researcher, spending 15+ years as an individual contributor before stepping into management. I also spent three years as a front-end engineer — which means I understand the implementation side of every design decision I make.

Leadership philosophy

I lead by delegating real ownership, not tasks. My job is to set a sharp vision, hire people smarter than me in their disciplines, and create the conditions where they do the best work of their careers. I stay close enough to lead by example when it matters — and far enough back to let the team surprise me.

Beyond the work

I teach, lecture, mentor, and coach designers and researchers at every career stage. I'm curriculum lead at GrowthTribe (5,000+ students), UXDX Barcelona Ambassador, board member at We Create Space, and founder of The Common Thread Club — a global supper club based in Barcelona. I've traveled to 70+ countries and I believe the best product insights come from living in the world, not just studying it.
