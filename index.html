Leadership Philosophy

Building teams that own the outcome

Leadership means setting a high bar and trusting the team to clear it — often exceeding it. The best design decisions don't come from the top of an org chart; they come from researchers, designers, and writers who are close to the user and empowered to act.

We build that environment through clear career frameworks, structured feedback culture, and shared ownership of metrics. When every team member understands how their work connects to retention, MAU, or revenue, the quality of decisions across the entire organization improves.

I lead by example: getting into the work when it matters, facilitating workshops with senior leaders, and modeling the craft standards we expect. But the goal is always to develop the next generation of design leaders — not to be the indispensable one in the room.
