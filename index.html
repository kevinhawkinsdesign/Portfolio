About me

The real design challenge is almost always organizational.

I've spent 20+ years building products at the intersection of behavioral science, product design, and business strategy — and most of that work has been about more than interfaces. It's getting the right people in the room, creating shared language between design, product, and engineering, and building the systems that let teams move fast without losing quality.

I started as a product designer and UX researcher, spent 15+ years as an IC, and even three years as a front-end engineer — so I understand the implementation side of every decision I make. I lead by delegating real ownership, hiring people smarter than me, and creating the conditions where they do the best work of their careers.

Away from the screen I teach and mentor, founded The Common Thread Club supper club in Barcelona, and have travelled to 70+ countries — because the best product insight comes from living in the world, not just studying it.
