How I lead

01

Data before opinion

Every design decision is tied to a measurable outcome — MAU, conversion, LTV — and tracked end-to-end with the right tools.

02

Teams build; leaders enable

I set the vision, clear the path, then step back. My job is to make the team around me demonstrably better.

03

Research is a business asset

User insight belongs in QBRs and roadmaps — not Notion docs. I build the infrastructure that makes that happen at scale.

04

Lead by example, always

Whether running a research sprint or writing design system principles, I show — not just tell.

05

Diversity compounds returns

The most effective product decisions come from teams that reflect the users they design for. It's a design quality issue.

06

Growth transfers to the team

Career frameworks, real ownership, and learning programs are the mechanism by which good teams become exceptional ones.
