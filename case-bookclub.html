BookClub · Director of Product Design · Oct 2020–Feb 2022

Building a social reading platform from zero as founding designer

Role
Director of Product Design
Scope
Full product — 0 to 1
Focus
Community · Author engagement · Reading UX

The situation

BookClub brings authors and readers together via online book clubs led by authors themselves. I joined as the founding designer and scaled to Director of Product Design, responsible for the entire design and research function from day one.

What we built

Starting from zero means making every foundational decision — information architecture, design system, interaction patterns, and team itself. The core challenge: making asynchronous community feel synchronous.

What I learned

The most valuable skill in 0-to-1 work isn't any particular method — it's the judgment to know which decisions matter enough to validate and which ones you just commit to.
