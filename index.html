About

I'm Kevin — a gay, Liberian-American product design executive based in Barcelona. 18 years building products and the teams behind them across the USA, Europe, and Asia. Currently VP of Product Experience at Monta, where I joined as their first-ever UX hire and built the practice from scratch.

Before Monta: Global UX Director at Glovo (100+ person org, 25+ countries), Director of Design at Amenitiz, UX Team Lead at Booking.com, and founding designer at BookClub. Earlier I led UX for Gap Inc. across all five sub-brands and customer loyalty, was the founding designer at Hello Alice (first virtual accelerator for women entrepreneurs, 13,000+ founders), and consulted for Capital One, PwC New Ventures, EY, Chase, and 50+ others through my own studio, KHD.

I'm also a curriculum lead and instructor who taught 5,000+ students at GrowthTribe and a guest lecturer at Georgetown University. I've given 52 public talks at conferences worldwide. I founded The Common Thread Club, a foodie community of 1,000+ in Barcelona. I sit on the board of We Create Space, serve as UXDX Barcelona Ambassador, and co-organize Hatch Conference Leadership Ateliers. I've visited 52 countries.

Leadership & Management

How I lead
teams and practice.

01 — Vision
I build practices, not just teams

At Monta (0→1), Amenitiz (rebuild), and Glovo (scale to 100+) I've established the vision, playbook, rituals, and culture that make design a strategic function. I formalize UX maturity from day one and integrate design insights into quarterly business reviews.

02 — People
I grow designers into leaders

Career frameworks, bi-weekly 1:1s, guest AMAs for professional development, first-hand user exposure, and cross-department collaboration. I distribute responsibility according to strengths and celebrate small wins — togetherness is the method, not just the outcome.

03 — Research
I chair executive research councils

At Glovo I rebuilt the research org post-layoffs with 9 senior researchers, implemented RallyUXR & Qualtrics for ReOps, and established UXR labs across EMEA. I recreated the prioritization process directly with COO, CPO, and Product Directors. At Amenitiz I led 3 strategic research trips with key accounts.

04 — Operations
I run design as a business

OKRs, budget, tooling, headcount modeling — design operations as craft. At Glovo I saved €500k in licensing while improving velocity. At Monta I own PostHog implementation and PLG comms. At Gap Inc. I grew BRIGHT Rewards from 0 to 2M members across five sub-brands.
