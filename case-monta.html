Context

Europe's #1 EV charging platform —
with no UX practice.

Monta had built a technically impressive product loved by early adopters — but as the company scaled toward mainstream EV drivers and enterprise operators, the absence of a UX function was becoming a growth constraint. The product had been built by engineers for early adopters. Now it needed to work for everyone.

I was hired as the first UX leader in the company's history. My remit: build the entire practice — team, process, culture, research, and product — while simultaneously shipping improvements to a live product used by hundreds of thousands of people.

Building the practice

From 0 to 20+ —
in under a year.

I hired and structured a 20+ person Product Experience team covering product design, UX research, and content design. I established the design system, the research ops infrastructure, the sprint rituals, the critique process, and the cross-functional ways of working that connected design directly to engineering and product management.

Critically, I built the team to be AI-native from day one — integrating AI tools into research synthesis, design generation, and product ideation as a standard practice rather than an experiment. This gave us dramatically faster iteration cycles and allowed a smaller team to punch well above its weight.

Product work

Monta Hub, Mobile 2.0,
and AI-native features.

Monta Hub — the operator dashboard used by charge point operators to manage their charging network. I led the UX for Hub's core flows: Dashboard, Payment & Billing, Customer Relations, and Transactions. The design system we built for Hub established the visual and interaction language now shared across all Monta products.

Mobile 2.0 — a ground-up redesign of the Monta driver app, rethinking how EV drivers discover chargers, start sessions, manage payments, and track their charging history across home, work, and public charging scenarios.

AI-native features — I'm leading design for Monta's AI product layer, including intelligent NOC (Network Operations Center) agents that help operators detect and resolve charging issues proactively, and AI-powered recommendations for drivers. This is active work — details NDA.
