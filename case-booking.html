Booking.com · UX Team Lead · Jul 2019–Feb 2021

Cross-brand UX for connected stay and global travel products

Role
UX Team Lead
Scope
Booking, Kayak, Priceline, Agoda
Focus
Connected Stay · Group travel · Local events

The situation

Booking Holdings operates Booking.com, Priceline, Agoda, Kayak, and OpenTable. I led two product teams focused on Connected Stay and cross-brand initiatives — designing cohesive experiences across brands, platforms, and user contexts.

What we built

Our team won two hackathons and shipped the ideas directly as product features: a group travel planner for multi-traveler coordination, and a local events hotel search surfacing accommodations based on nearby events.

What I learned

Cross-brand work requires finding underlying user needs that manifest differently on each surface. That's what makes it coherent rather than just visually consistent.
