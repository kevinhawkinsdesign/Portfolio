Kevin Hawkins is a design leader who builds empowered teams and products people love.

VP of UX

I lead with empathy and ambition. I oversee product design, user research, content design, localization, and product operations — connecting design directly to business value at every level.

Player-Coach

Strategy and craft are not separate. I set direction in the boardroom and still ship UI. The best leaders stay close to the work — you can't lead what you don't understand.

AI-Native

AI is a design material, not a feature. I build with it, research with it, and design products powered by it. Being AI-native means integrating intelligence into the practice from day one.

Human-First

I don't design for users — I design for humans. Emotional, irrational, wonderful humans. Empathy is the most important design skill, and I cultivate it in everything I do.

About

I'm Kevin — a gay, Liberian-American design leader based in Barcelona with 18 years of building products and the teams behind them across Europe, the US, and Asia. Currently VP of Product Experience at Monta, where I was their first-ever UX hire — I built the entire practice from scratch.

I've managed award-winning teams of up to 105 people, launched products used by millions, and scaled design organizations through every phase of growth. I've been a founding designer, a global director, and a consultant — I've seen what works from every seat at the table.

Outside work I founded The Common Thread Club, a foodie community of 1,000+ in Barcelona, and serve as UXDX Barcelona Ambassador. I sit on the board of We Create Space. I've visited 52 countries — each one taught me something about designing for the full range of human experience.

Leadership & Management

How I lead
teams and practice.

01 — Vision
I build design practices, not just design teams

At every company I've joined, I've established the vision, playbook, rituals, and culture that make design a strategic function — not just a delivery arm. I've done this at Monta (0→1), Amenitiz (rebuild), and Glovo (scale).

02 — People
I grow designers into design leaders

I've built career frameworks, coaching programs, and mentoring cultures that create clear paths forward. My teams consistently describe me as someone who invests genuinely in their growth — not just their output.

03 — Research
I chair executive research councils

I've led market research councils at the C-suite level, giving design a seat at the strategic table with data to back it. At Glovo I created an empowered 9-person research team that supported 20M monthly transactions across 30+ countries.

04 — Operations
I run design as a business

From budget planning to tooling decisions, OKRs to headcount modeling — I treat design operations as a craft. I saved Glovo over €500k in licensing costs while improving team velocity and output quality simultaneously.
