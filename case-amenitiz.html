The situation

Amenitiz had strong product-market fit in core European markets but needed to scale internationally across 60+ countries and 15,000+ properties without degrading the experience. There was no research practice, no localization framework, and no design leadership. I was brought in to build those foundations.

What we built

We recruited a full team of six designers in the first 45 days and launched immediately into an innovation workshop with Product and Engineering. Before touching the product, I led three field research trips to key accounts — the single best investment of those early months.

We redesigned the three most critical UIs: calendar, self-service onboarding, and inventory management. I personally led the Brazilian market launch — new language, personalized product, streamlined PLG signup with no sales assist.

What I learned

Moving fast doesn't mean skipping the research. The field trips surfaced problems no analytics dashboard would have shown, and built instant credibility with product and engineering.
